What is Physics

What is physics? Physics is a science that describes other sciences such as Astronomy, Biology, Chemistry, Engineering Meteorology, and so on. Everything on our planet, solar system, galaxy, and the Universe interacts and moves and forces share a huge chunk in that. Physics is the study of forces and interactions of all of those topics.
