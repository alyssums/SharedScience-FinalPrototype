About Shared Science!

The aim of Shared Science is to provide educational resources to make science more engaging and fun. We chose the topics of astronomy, physics, and chemistry and put them in a format that kids could enjoy. To motivate kids to learn about science can help them make decisions based on their own reasoning in the near future.

Shared Science began in October 2014 as a product of a Computer Information Systems course at Cal Poly Pomona to help kids develop an educational knowledge and a fun way to learn.
