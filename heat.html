Heat

Heat can basically do everything, it can move from one place to another, increase energyand even get atoms excited. Heat is energy. When a system is increase by heat, it really means that the increase of the amount of energy in that system. Thermodynamics is related to heat. When you talk about thermodynamics, you are talking about the measure of energy moving in and out of a system.
