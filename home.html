Welcome to Shared Science

Shared Science is a website that allows children to improve their scientific knowledge by providing them with information on various scientific subjects. This includes articles, videos, and instructions on how to do their own scientific experiments. Our website will provide kids with a basic understanding of scientific topics. We hope to make science more fun and interesting for kids so that they will be more enthusiastic about learning.
