Welcome!

Shared Science is a website that allows children to improve their scientific knowledge by providing them with information on various scientific subjects. This includes articles, videos, and instructions on how to do their own scientific experiments. Our website will provide kids with a basic understanding of scientific topics. We hope to make science more fun and interesting for kids. Kids will be more enthusiastic about learning.

Topics

Astronomy:
Study the topics of planets, moons, stars, space, solar systems and galaxies.
Physics:
Learn about the topics of electricity, gravity, light, sound and much more.
Chemistry:
Get to know more on atoms, elements, metals, solids, gases and liquids.

Experiments

Experiments are amazing for kids to engage in a fun and exciting way to discover and test hypothesis.
